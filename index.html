About

I am currently pursuing the integrated M.S./Ph.D. program at KAIST. I work in the Geometric Structures Lab under the supervision of Prof. Suhyoung Choi.

My current interests lie in low-dimensional geometry, especially geometric structures on manifolds and orbifolds, deformation spaces, and Lie-group representations. In particular, I am interested in real projective, affine, and flat Lorentz structures.

Research Interests

My current research interests are shaped by the themes of the Geometric Structures Lab.

  • Geometric structures on low-dimensional manifolds and orbifolds
  • Deformation spaces of (G, X)-structures
  • Representations of surface and 2-orbifold fundamental groups into Lie groups
  • Real projective, affine, and flat Lorentz structures
